## Non-Custodial Vault Infrastructure

rgUSD deposits are held in non-custodial smart contract accounts. Ranger cannot withdraw user funds directly.

Strategy managers can allocate capital to whitelisted protocols using pre-approved functions. All management actions are restricted by smart contract guardrails.
