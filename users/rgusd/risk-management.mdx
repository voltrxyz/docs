---
title: Risk Management
description: Understanding and mitigating risks in rgUSD strategies
---

All DeFi strategies involve risk. Smart contracts can have vulnerabilities, protocols can fail, and market conditions can change rapidly. We've identified the key risks associated with rgUSD and implemented specific mitigations for each. Understanding these risks is essential before depositing.

| Risk Factor | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|---|
| **Protocol Risk** | Only lending protocols with liquidity depth, operational resilience and robust liquidation mechanisms integrated. |
| **Cross-Platform Risk** | Capital is diversified across multiple vetted lending protocols, reducing single-point-of-failure risk. |
| **Transparency & Operational Risk** | All positions visible on-chain; live dashboard with health, basis, and PnL metrics. |
