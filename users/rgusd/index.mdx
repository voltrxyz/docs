## Overview

Ranger USD (rgUSD) is a liquid yield-bearing token designed to deliver simple one-click yield from institutional trading strategies. It is a yield aggregator that dynamically routes capital across Solana's best lending markets to deliver consistent higher lending returns with instant withdrawals.

rgUSD holders automatically earn yield without needing to lock funds or manage complex DeFi positions.

## Key Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Withdrawal Terms">
    Instant redemptions. Your rgUSD converts to USDC immediately with no lock-up periods.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Management Fees">
    Zero management fees. There is a 50% outperformance share, which is a fee from the excess yield rgUSD generates above the second-best lending opportunity in the ecosystem.
